About Cuba

Cuba is a beautiful island off the coast of Florida that is about the size of Pennsylvania. The island is divided into 15 diverse provinces that is home to 11 million residents. Although Spanish is the vernacular, worry not! English is spoken is many of the provinces as well. Not only is Cuba appealing to the eye, it is environmentally conscious and is the leader in sustainable ecological practices. Come to Cuba to not only relax, but to explore history and culture!

Destinations and Activities

  1. Neighborhoods of Havana
    • In the neighobrhoods of Havana you can find a lot of Spanish architecture, famous ice cream, and an authentic Cuban music experience.
  2. Western Cuba
    • Western Cuba is within mountain ranges that are home to many tobacco and sugarcane fields.
  3. Central Cuba
    • If you are looking for some beachside relaxation, Central Cuba is the place to go, considering it is home to many gorgeous beaches!
  4. Eastern Cuba
    • If you really want to experience Cuban culture, visit eastern Cuba. Here you will find many Cuban festivals!
